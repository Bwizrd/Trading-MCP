# Quick Reference Guide

## Signal Conditions

### BUY Signal (Long)
```
1. All 4 stochastics < 20 (oversold)
2. Fast stochastic crosses above 20
3. Time filter: 14:30-21:00 UTC (optional)
4. Trend filter: 10 pips range in 10 bars (optional)
```

### SELL Signal (Short)
```
1. All 4 stochastics > 80 (overbought)
2. Fast stochastic crosses below 80
3. Time filter: 14:30-21:00 UTC (optional)
4. Trend filter: 10 pips range in 10 bars (optional)
```

## Default Parameters

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Fast Stochastic | 9-3 |
| Med-Fast Stochastic | 14-3 |
| Med-Slow Stochastic | 40-4 |
| Slow Stochastic | 60-10 |
| Oversold Level | 20 |
| Overbought Level | 80 |
| Stop Loss | 15 pips |
| Take Profit | 25 pips |

## Best Timeframes

- ✅ 15 minutes
- ✅ 30 minutes
- ✅ 1 hour
- ⚠️ 5 minutes (more noise)
- ❌ 1 minute (too noisy)

## Color Coding

- **Blue**: Fast (9-period)
- **Green**: Med-Fast (14-period)
- **Orange**: Med-Slow (40-period)
- **Red**: Slow (60-period)
